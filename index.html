About SAI

What if your skincare routinecould understand your day?

Meaning of SAI

SAI is inspired by the Korean word“사이/saɪ/”, meaning “between”.

SAI stands for Self-care Assistant Interface.

The name reflects SAI’s role as a soft interface between daily signals and skincare choices.

01 Between wellness and skincare
02 Between environmental changes and daily care
03 Between the products you own and the way you use them
04 Between beauty devices and the right timing

Why More Products Make Care Feel Less Clear

Most of us do not lack products. There is a serum for glow, a cream for barrier care, a sunscreen for protection, and a device waiting to be used. But having more does not always make the next step clearer. Some days, the question is not “What should I add?” It is “What actually makes sense today?”

Using what you already own well can be more meaningful than adding another step. It may mean choosing one product with more purpose. Letting a device rest. Or keeping the routine simple because the day already felt heavy. Care does not always have to be more. It can be more connected.

In SAI, this idea appears through product registration and daily routine guidance.
